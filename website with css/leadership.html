LEADERSHIP


Students are given a wide range of opportunities to develop and practice leadership skills in preparation for life beyond school.Many Student
Leadership activities will focus on improving the quality of learning and teaching in the classroom, supporting the school’s aim to be a place
where everyone enjoys learning and is creative – trying new things, finding solutions and embracing their responsibilities. The school recognises
the need and importance of games and sports activities for the development of leadership qualities in the child and provide variety of games and
sports facilities for the same.

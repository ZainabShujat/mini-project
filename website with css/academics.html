Academics

Curriculum Overview

Primary School (Grades I-V)

  • Core Subjects: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Environmental Studies
  • Additional Areas: Art and Craft, Music and Dance, Physical Education, Computer Education

Middle School (Grades VI-VIII)

  • Core Subjects: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
  • Third Language Options: Sanskrit, French, German
  • Additional Areas: Computer Science, Visual and Performing Arts, Physical and Health Education

Secondary School (Grades IX-X)

  • Core Subjects: English, Second Language (Hindi/French/German), Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Science (History, Geography, Political Science, Economics)
  • Additional Areas: Information Technology, Physical Education, Art Education

Senior Secondary School (Grades XI-XII)

  • Science Stream: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, English, Elective Subject (Computer Science/Economics/Physical Education)
  • Commerce Stream: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, English, Elective Subject (Mathematics/Information Practices/Physical Education)
  • Humanities Stream: History, Political Science, Sociology/Psychology, English, Elective Subject (Economics/Geography/Physical Education)

Holistic Development Programs

  • Value Education: Regular sessions aimed at instilling moral and ethical values.
  • Life Skills Education: Workshops and activities designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.
  • Co-Curricular Activities: Debate and Literary Club, Science Club, Eco Club, Music and Dance Society, Art and Craft Club
  • Sports and Physical Education: Facilities and training for sports such as Basketball, Football, Cricket, Athletics, Yoga

Technology Integration

  • Smart Classrooms: Equipped with interactive boards and audio-visual aids.
  • Computer Labs: State-of-the-art labs with internet access to facilitate ICT education.
  • ATL Lab (Atal Tinkering Lab): A space dedicated to fostering innovation and creativity among students through hands-on projects in science and technology.

Assessment and Evaluation

The school follows a continuous and comprehensive evaluation system that assesses both scholastic and co-scholastic areas, ensuring a balanced approach to student development.

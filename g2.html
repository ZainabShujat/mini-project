FOUNDER’S DAY


On the occasion of the birthday of Dr Ashok K. Chauhan, Founder President, Amity Universe celebrated as Founder’s Day on November 4, 2014, Dr (Mrs)
Amita Chauhan, Chairperson Amity Group of Schools & RBEF felicitated 10 CGPA achievers of Class X, scholarship holders from Class V to VIII, stream and
subject toppers of Class XII with certificates, trophies, badges and scholarships. School Principal Sunila Athley commended the achievements of the
students.
